Tobacco and Nicotine Education

If you're unfamiliar with terms, please look below!

Terminology

Prevention
Stopping the usage of tobacco or nicotine before it starts
Cessation
Commonly known as "quiting". Stopping the usage of tobacco or nicotine after it has already started
Cold Turkey
Common Term. Cessation, or quitting, without the use of cessation aids like nicotine gum, patches, lozenges, etc..
